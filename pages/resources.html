PLAY: Review outputs of Customer & Product Discovery

Customer Discovery delivers the following outputs:

  • The "to-be" customer journey (customer pain points being solved and gains being made)
  • Customer KPIs that measure the pain points removed, features delivered or value added to the customer

Product Discovery delivers the following outputs: 

  • Product backlog, which is a list of product features and user stories
  • Product roadmap, which is a value delivery plan on a rough timeline
  • Product KPIs that measure the performance of a product and the business value delivered
  • Business processes that are created or updated along with the enterprise capabilities that support the business process, documented in Business Process to Enterprise Capabilities Map (artifact 1) and the Enterprise Capability further documented hierarchically in Enterprise Capabilities to Applications Map (artifact #2)

PLAY: Conduct Technical Discovery
  1. Review the current technical solution, known defects and limitations
  2. Review app components using the EA repository/application inventory
  3. Conduct technical deep-dive sessions with the team. Document findings.
  4. Use Reference Architecture including the "how-to" guidance, the EA directives, and call on the EA Advisory for issue-specific guidance (at any time during Tech Discovery)
  5. Consider potential solutions
  6. Conduct multi-criteria analysis w/ scrum team(s) for potential options (tech, platform, skills, resources, sizing, timeline, risks)
