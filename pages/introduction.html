This site aims to shed light on key aspects of various lifecycles: project management, technology lifecycles, technology adoption, and software development. If you're dealing with projects or technology in any capacity, SDLC. It's primary focus is on various topics related to software development, is a work in progress, and isn't yet well organized.

The ideas found here represent those acquired over over twenty-five years of working with software solutions to business critical data, processes, and operations.

So, feel free to browse through and use what you find useful. The aim is to offer a useful, no-frills perspective to these many topics. Thanks for visiting!
