Play Name:

Technology Play

Purpose:

The purpose of the "Technology Play" is to analyze, evaluate, and select the appropriate technology stack and tools for the project. This ensures that the technology used aligns with the project's goals, scalability requirements, and complies with organizational standards.

Checklist:

  1. Technology Stack Assessment: Evaluate various technology stacks based on project needs and goals.

  2. Compatibility Check: Ensure that the chosen technology is compatible with existing systems and future scalability.

  3. Governance and Compliance: Verify that the chosen technologies align with governance policies and compliance requirements.

  4. Cost-Benefit Analysis: Perform a cost-benefit analysis to ensure that the technology provides value for the investment.

  5. Security Review: Conduct a security assessment to identify any potential vulnerabilities.

  6. Team Expertise: Consider the skillset of the development team and how well it aligns with the chosen technology.

  7. Pilot Testing: Run a small-scale test to validate the technology choices before full-scale implementation.

  8. Documentation: Document the technology choices, reasons for selection, and any potential risks.

  9. Stakeholder Communication: Ensure that key stakeholders are informed and in agreement with the technology choices made.

Key Questions:

  • What are the technology requirements for this project?

  • How does the chosen technology align with organizational and project goals?

  • Are there any governance or compliance issues related to the technology selection?

  • What are the costs involved, and do they justify the benefits?

  • Are there any security vulnerabilities to be aware of?

Primary Utility:

  • Focus: This play hones in on the critical technology-related aspects of the project, providing clear directives to align technology choices with project objectives.

  • Flexibility: It offers a modular guide to technology selection that can be adapted depending on project or organizational needs.

  • Cohesion: By adhering to the steps and insights of this play, stakeholders ensure technology decisions are cohesive with the broader objectives outlined in the "Purpose & Discovery Playbook."

The Technology Play is essential for making informed, effective decisions about technology use in the project. It serves as a robust guide that reduces risks related to technology choices, ensuring alignment with both business goals and governance frameworks.
