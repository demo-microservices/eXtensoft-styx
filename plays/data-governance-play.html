Data Governance Play

Name & Purpose:

  • Name: Data Governance Play
  • Purpose: To establish and maintain high data quality, data security, and compliance with relevant regulations. This play aims to ensure that data is handled responsibly and ethically throughout its lifecycle.

Checklist:

  1. Identify and classify types of data involved in the project.
  2. Establish data ownership and stewardship roles.
  3. Review and apply relevant data governance laws and regulations.
  4. Define data quality criteria and metrics.
  5. Set up data access controls and encryption measures.
  6. Implement data backup and recovery plans.
  7. Conduct regular audits for data quality and compliance.
  8. Document data governance policies, procedures, and audit trails.

Key Questions:

  • What types of data are we collecting, storing, and processing?
  • Who is responsible for each aspect of data governance?
  • What laws and regulations must we comply with?
  • How do we measure and maintain data quality?
  • What security measures are in place for data protection?

Utility:

  • Focus: Provides structured guidance on how to manage various aspects of data, ensuring high quality, security, and legal compliance.
  • Flexibility: This play can be adapted to different data environments and regulatory contexts.
  • Cohesion: Adherence to this play ensures that all stakeholders are aligned in safeguarding and managing data as a valuable asset.
