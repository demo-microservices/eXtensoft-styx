Quality Assurance Play

Name & Purpose:
Quality Assurance Play - To ensure the highest level of quality in the delivered software through comprehensive testing.

Checklist:

  1. Identify critical software components and functions.
  2. Define test strategies for unit tests, integration tests, and end-to-end tests.
  3. Prepare test data and test environments.
  4. Execute tests and track results.
  5. Implement code reviews.

Key Questions:

  1. What are the quality objectives for this project?
  2. How are the different types of testing coordinated?
  3. Who is responsible for each aspect of quality assurance?
  4. How are defects tracked and managed?
  5. What metrics are in place to measure quality?
