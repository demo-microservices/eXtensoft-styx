Migration Planning Play

Name & Purpose:

Name: Migration Planning Play
Purpose: To ensure a smooth transition of users and data to the new or upgraded system. This play focuses on planning for user migration, data migration, Verification & Validation (V&V), and communication to stakeholders.

Checklist:

  1. Identify the existing data that needs to be migrated, and determine the most appropriate data migration methods.
  2. Plan for user transition, including user training and support materials.
  3. Develop Verification & Validation (V&V) procedures to ensure that data and functions have been accurately transferred.
  4. Create a comprehensive communication plan to inform stakeholders of migration activities, schedules, and what to expect during the transition.

Key Questions:

  1. What data needs to be migrated, and what are the appropriate methods for doing so?
  2. How will users be transitioned to the new or upgraded system? Are there training or support materials in place?
  3. What Verification & Validation (V&V) steps are planned to confirm the integrity of the migrated data and functions?
  4. What is the communication plan to keep stakeholders informed before, during, and after migration?

The Migration Planning Play is essential for mitigating the complexities and risks associated with moving data and users to a new or upgraded system. A smooth migration process minimizes disruptions and ensures continued alignment with business goals. This play is a crucial component of the Planning & Risk Mitigation Playbook, providing targeted guidelines for one of the most critical stages of any software development project.
