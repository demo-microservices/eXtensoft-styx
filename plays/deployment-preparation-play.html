Deployment Preparation Play

  • Name & Purpose: Deployment Preparation Play prepares the team and the software for deployment into a staging or production environment.

  • Checklist:

    • Verify all code is merged and tested
    • Prepare staging environment
    • Backup current production system
    • Finalize deployment schedule

  • Key Questions:

    • Is the codebase ready for deployment?
    • Have backups been made of the current system?
    • What is the final deployment schedule?
