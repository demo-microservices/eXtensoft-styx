Play Name:

Governance Play

Purpose:

The purpose of the "Governance Play" is to establish the rules, policies, and decision-making frameworks that will guide the project. This includes identifying relevant regulations, ensuring compliance, and establishing clear roles and responsibilities for governance oversight.

Checklist:

  1. Identify Governance Bodies: Establish which internal and external governance bodies have oversight for this project.

  2. Regulatory Landscape: Understand all laws, regulations, and corporate policies that may affect the project.

  3. Roles and Responsibilities: Clearly define who is responsible for governance-related tasks and decision-making.

  4. Compliance Mechanisms: Implement tools and practices to ensure ongoing compliance with regulations and policies.

  5. Decision Framework: Create a decision-making process that considers risk, compliance, and business value.

  6. Documentation and Record-keeping: Document all governance activities, decisions, and compliance checks.

  7. Stakeholder Communication: Ensure that all stakeholders are aware of governance processes and outcomes.

  8. Audit Preparedness: Establish routines for internal and external audits to assess governance and compliance.

  9. Review and Update: Regularly review the governance framework and make updates as necessary.

Key Questions:

  • What are the regulatory requirements affecting this project?

  • Who has the ultimate authority for various types of project decisions?

  • How will compliance be monitored and maintained?

  • What are the consequences of non-compliance?

  • How will governance activities be documented and communicated?

Primary Utility:

  • Focus: The play provides a focused approach to governance, ensuring that the project aligns with legal, ethical, and corporate frameworks.

  • Flexibility: This play can be adapted for different projects and organizational needs, serving as a modular guide for governance.

  • Cohesion: By following the Governance Play, all stakeholders will operate under a unified framework, contributing to the broader objectives of the "Purpose & Discovery Playbook."

The Governance Play serves to mitigate the risks associated with legal non-compliance, misalignment with organizational policies, and inadequate oversight. It is crucial for establishing a robust governance framework that ensures long-term project sustainability and integrity.
