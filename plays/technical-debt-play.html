Technical Debt Play

Name & Purpose:
Technical Debt Play - To manage and minimize the accumulation of technical debt over the lifecycle of a project.

Checklist:

  1. Identify existing areas of technical debt.
  2. Prioritize technical debt items.
  3. Establish a schedule for debt repayment.
  4. Track new debt items as they are introduced.
  5. Update stakeholders on the status of technical debt management.

Key Questions:

  1. What is the current state of technical debt in the project?
  2. How are technical debt items prioritized?
  3. What is the repayment plan?
  4. How is new technical debt prevented or minimized?
  5. How are stakeholders kept informed of technical debt status?
