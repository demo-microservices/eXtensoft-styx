This site is a brain dump. It consists of lessons learned from 2+ decades of designing, constructing, maintaining multiple enterprise software systems in the Insurance (Policy,Claims,Actuarial), Medical Infomatics, Digital Asset Management, and other domains. These systems were designed and constructed based upon antecedent applications, necessitating smooth transitions of data & users, continuity of service to adjacent systems.

Technology migrations included Mainframe to Distributed, Monolith to SOA, Monolith to Microservices, CRUD based to CQRS and Event Sourcing, VB to C#, ColdFusion to C#, tradition request-response website to Single Page Application.

UX applications include websites using ASP, ASP.NET Webforms, ASP.NET MVC, Razor Pages, Javascript based Single Page Application, Windows Forms, and WPF.

ITIL, Waterfall, Agile, and peculiar combinations have been encountered, resulting in lessons learned from a variety of SOPs and Methodologies. Over time, some concepts, processes, and practices consistently evinced their utility, and others fell by the wayside.
